Hi there! My name is Tracey and my little pet project is called Bobbins and Beads.
For the past 30 years I have been a primary school teacher and latterly a deputy head, but in my precious spare time I love sewing and crafting. It brings me great joy and helps to me to be calm and relaxed.
Ever since I took O Level needlework at school I have had a passion for sewing and dressmaking. I love making my own clothes.
Recently I decided to branch out and began to create other items using my sewing skills, such as bags, purses and other handmade items . I have also developed an interest in making jewellery using beautiful beads.
So that’s where my name Bobbins and Beads fits in. I have put together a selection of my handmade items together on this website. I hope you enjoy browsing and maybe you might even like to purchase something too.
